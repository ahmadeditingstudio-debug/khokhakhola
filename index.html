Our Story

A Legacy of
Authentic Flavour

Khoka Khola began in 2018 as a small khoka with one pot and one dream — to bring grandmother's recipes to Islamabad. Today, our Blue Area restaurant honours that same spirit.

Every spice is sourced from the markets of Lahore and Peshawar. Every recipe is tested against memory — the memory of Sunday lunches, street-food evenings, and family dastarkhans.
