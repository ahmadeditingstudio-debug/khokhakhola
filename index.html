Our Story

A Taste of
Istanbul

MêZ is Islamabad's home for authentic Turkish cuisine, nestled in the heart of F-6 Markaz. We bring the warmth, craft, and soul of Istanbul's legendary food culture directly to your table.

From charcoal-grilled kebabs to hand-rolled baklava, every dish is prepared fresh daily using imported Turkish spices and time-honoured techniques passed down through generations.
